FAQ

PayPAI supports registration/login via email or Web3 wallet:
  • Email: Register or log in using your email and a verification code. (If you don't receive the code, please check your spam folder or request a new one.)
  • Wallet: Connect an existing Web3 wallet to register or log in, e.g., MetaMask.

PayPAl supports the following mainnets and tokens. You can also manually enter a token contractaddress when making a payment.
  • Ethereum: ETH/USDC/USDT
  • Base: ETH/USDC/USDT
  • BNB Chain: BNB/USDC/USDT/BUSD
To perform payment operations, gas fees are required.

There are two ways to activate PayPAI functions:
  • Direct Input: Simply type the desired operation directly in the chat, such as Direct Pay, Cooling-off Pay, or Batch Pay.
  • Quick Menu: Enter “/” in the chat input to trigger the quick command menu and choose a function.
